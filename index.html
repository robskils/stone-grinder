Somewhere along the line, we all gave up on grinders - cheap, disposable, forgettable. We think there is a better way. So we started over: a single piece of surgical steel, with a silhouette engineered to sit in the palm like a cold, perfect pebble.

More than the finest herb grinder on the market, it is a subtle, beautiful reinvention of one - a tool in the purest sense, made to be used forever, and an object of beauty to live with. You are not just buying a grinder. You are investing in a piece of design built to last.

01 - The Object

The S-Curve.

↳ Geometry

A four-lobe sweep

The swooping fidget-spinner profile is not styling. It is leverage. Dense herb yields without effort, and grip issues disappear into 10mm pillowed edges.

↳ The Teeth

Thirty diamonds that slice

Three concentric rings of solid steel elongated-diamond teeth, 6.5mm tall. They cleave the material cleanly rather than crushing it - preserving every trichome.

↳ The Glide

A frictionless track

A perfectly sealed internal sliding bearing on a 21mm radius. It turns with the quiet, weighted inevitability of a vault door.

↳ The Mass

Heavy, by design

A hollow internal lattice sheds dead weight while a heft remains where it belongs. It feels, unmistakably, like something that cost more than it did.

One finish, no compromise

No options, no variants, no decisions. One design classic - take it or leave it. A flawless polished chrome that throws the room back at you: the showpiece, and the one you leave on the table.

02 - The Stash Tin

The box is the point.

Every other grinder's packaging ends up in the bin. Ours becomes the most over-engineered stash jar ever made - solid metal, airtight, and yours forever.

Explore the stash tin →

Magnetic snap-lock

Eight embedded neodymium magnets - four in the lid, four in the base - pull the custom silhouette into perfect alignment and close with a single, satisfying report.

Smell-proof double seal

A raised registration lip and a premium double-wiper silicone O-ring. 100% airtight. 100% waterproof.

Terpene shield

A hidden friction-fit pocket in the roof holds a Boveda 62% pack. Your herb stays exactly as cured.

Total UV blackout

Solid metal walls. No light gets in, so nothing degrades. Suspended on cut EVA foam.

Zero waste

Nothing here is meant to be thrown away. The packaging is the product. That is the whole idea.
