Geometry

A four-lobe sweep, built for leverage

The swooping fidget-spinner profile is not styling. It is leverage. Those four lobes give your fingers real purchase, so dense, sticky herb yields without a fight and grip issues disappear entirely.

Massive 10mm radial fillets round every top and bottom edge, turning hard steel into something pillowy and pebble-like in the hand. You feel it before you understand it.

10mm radial fillets · pillowy hand feel

The Cut

Thirty diamond teeth that slice

Thirty solid-steel, elongated-diamond teeth stand 6.5mm tall, arranged across three concentric rings. They are shaped to meet material side-on and cleave it cleanly, rather than bruising it between two flat plates.

A clean slice keeps the trichomes intact - the frosted resin heads where aroma and potency actually live - so what you grind holds its colour, structure, and smell.

Slices, never crushes

The Glide

A frictionless, sealed bearing

The two halves turn on a perfectly sealed internal sliding bearing, machined to a 21mm radius. There is no play, no catch, no grit - just the slow, weighted certainty of a vault door swinging shut.

Inside, a hollow metal lattice with 4mm structural walls sheds dead weight while preserving the heavy, premium heft exactly where the hand wants it. Balanced, not merely heavy.

21mm radius · 4mm internal lattice
